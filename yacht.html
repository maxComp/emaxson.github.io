Idaho Yacht Club to erect building

Idaho Statesman July 8, 1937

MCCALL-Payette lake added a yacht club Wednesday to its recreation development program. Construction of a two-story fire-proof concrete building on the lake front in McCall to house facilities of the Idaho Yacht club, a corporation of Invitational membership, was begun under direction of five Incorporators. The managers are Ernest. Miller of Boise, president; Carl Brown of McCall, R. W. Jones of Ontario, Ore., William C. Woller of Boise, and Wylle Beall of Boise, secretary.

Beall said the building would be finished by the middle of August. A crew of 12 at work under supervision of I. C. Whiteley, McCall contractor.

The building will have a 70-foot front on McCall's main street and extend 80 feet toward the lake approximately 30 feet from the waterline. In the basement will be space for storage of motor boats, rowboats, sailboats, and other craft. There will be a machine shop. A marine track will provide easy transportation of boats from shop to lake. A pier extending Into the water will be used for temporary mooring of boats. A swimmers dock will be constructed.

Place for Sunbathing
On the first floor of the club will be a lounge, kitchen, rest rooms, and club-managed stores for sale of outing equipment. On the rear deck above the basement will be a lighthouse 25 feet high in which - there will be a circular lounge fitted with red-leather couches and chairs trimmed in chromium.

Second deck of the lighthouse will contain a light with an open deckwalk. A spiral stairway will extend from the lighthouse into the basement. Front of the club will be on a raised elevation for a large promenade surrounded with a pipe railing built as nearly as possible like a ship's rail. This upper deck, Beall said, will be used for sunbathing, for views of the lake and surounding country, and for general visiting among club members. Lunch service will be available. Uniformed attendants will be on hand.

Individual lockers and sets of showers for men and women will be Installed in the basement.

Boats to be rented
Building and equipment is being leased to the Idaho Yacht club by the Wie-Bell corporation of Boise. Color scheme of the club house will be white trimmed in gold. Three front windows will be portholes.

'The club will be strictly for members only,' Beall said. 'Membership will be carefully selected. It is the object of the corporation to provide a reasonably exclusive facility for a group that wants to motor, fish and swim at Payette lake, boats of all types will be sold and kept for hire.'

In winter, Beall said, the club will be a rendezvous for skiers and skaters. A rink will be provided at the club. Interior space will be 2500 square feet of floor. Open deck space will be 5600 square feet. The rear deck will be built to accommodate dancing,

Insignia of the yacht club is an Interlocked TYC in red, white and blue against a salden anchor.
