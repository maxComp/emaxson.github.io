Engineer O. R. Price Tells of Work on Section Above Emmett

Idaho Statesman August 2, 1912

O. R. Price of Smith's Ferry, station engineer for the Idaho Northern railroad, was in Bolse yesterday attending to matters of business connected with the road.

"The grading of the new road from Emmett to the Payette lake is now practically completed as far as Smith's Ferry," said Mr. Price. The track has been built as far as Horseshoe Bend, but only construction trains are being run on that portion of the road. Regular train service as far as Smith's Ferry will probably be established by November 1. "Excavation work for the new line has been begun as far as McCall, but the grading has not been completed to that place."
